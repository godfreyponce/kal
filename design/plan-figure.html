Plan — “Figure”

The profile as a living 3D clay figure — this one is real: drag to rotate it, tap a floating stat chip (or the body itself) and the editor below switches to that region. Built from geometric primitives in code (no model file), matte clay under soft studio light, slow idle turn. three.js from CDN, ~92 KB gzip, loaded only on this screen.

Meals follow your Variant-B pick (form-swap editing, now with food photos); Memory follows Variant A. Steppers and memory deletion are live here too.

New this round: separator dots gone; the weight editor shows a live trend chart (hover it) with the goal as a line, no deadline; meal saves choose just today (the ⇄ override chat already uses) or every day; a “not in groceries? ask kal” hand-off; and “use my photos” marks where your generated 3D model plugs in — tap it for the how.
