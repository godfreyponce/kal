Plan in the silent-menu language

3 variants, issue #28 round 4 of 4, cream canvas per round 1. #30 and #31 were open and unbuilt at drawing time, so these show today's Plan, not their redesigns. WebGL figure is out of scope (placeholder shown).

A condensed scroll of the Plan screen: header, profile figure, this week's adherence, meal plan, memory. V1 goes full silent menu: cream section bands, uppercase meal names with kcal and protein pills, and the adherence cells recolored into the palette (hit caramel, miss red-brown, today chocolate). V2 restyles the furniture but leaves the data viz alone: same bands and pills, adherence and trend keep today's green/red/terracotta coding. V3 is the editorial hybrid: serif titles survive inside the cream bands, palette swaps, data viz recolored like V1. All keep the enter stagger with the reduced-motion off-switch.
