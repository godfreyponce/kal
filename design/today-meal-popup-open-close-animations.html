Kal — design exploration, round 3

Opening & closing the meal card

Same card everywhere (your picked B + B3, last grey line removed) — only the open/close motion differs. Every variant now has a real exit animation, not just an entrance. Tap a meal to open; close with ✕, the scrim, or Esc. Open and close a few times in each phone — the feel is the whole decision. Timings are in the CSS, one block per variant, easy to tweak.

Animation 1
Soft fade
What's built today, completed with a mirrored exit: fade + gentle scale from 96%, and on close it recedes the same way, slightly faster (0.18s in, 0.15s out). Calm and invisible — motion you feel but never notice.

Animation 2
Spring pop
Playful: the card pops in from 90% with a small overshoot past full size before settling (0.34s spring), and shrinks away quickly on close (0.13s). The most "alive" of the three — motion you do notice, in a good way.

Animation 3
Rise & sink
iOS-sheet energy without the sheet: the card drifts up ~26px into place on open, and sinks back down as it fades on close. Directional motion — feels like the card came from the meal list and returns to it.
