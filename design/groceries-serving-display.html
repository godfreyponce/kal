“My serving” on grocery cards

The macros on each card scale to the amount you actually aim for, shown oz-first. Chicken and rice have a stored cooked↔raw yield, so their amount is tappable — tap it below to flip. Macros don’t change when flipped: 6 oz cooked and 8 oz raw are the same food. (Storage approaches A and B render identically — this is the shared visible result.)
