Groceries — design rework

3 variants · issue #1 · direction: Chipotle iOS food treatment (owner, 2026-07-15)

Shared direction across all three: free-floating food photography (no card containers — white product shots melt into the cream via mix-blend-mode: multiply), generous whitespace, bold uppercase editorial type, warm brown/cream palette, small pill badges, elevated rounded CTA. Serving pills are tappable ⇄ flips (chicken sits on two shelves — flipping one flips both, same as prod). Photo-less foods (olive oil, broccoli) show the fallback treatment: category-tinted disc + serif initial. The add/edit form and lookup flow are unchanged this round — they inherit the winner's palette only. Motion per docs/design/motion-and-feel.md: staggered spring settle on load, scale(.97) press.
Variant A — Menu Board
The current one-row-per-item list translated into Chipotle's menu language: photos float free at left, hairline rules instead of card borders, heavy uppercase names, cocoa-on-cream. Densest of the three; closest to what shipped, so the safest migration.
